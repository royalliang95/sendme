Overview

The cute otter is designed with a big smile on his face, expressing pure joy and relaxation. The beachy vibes are further emphasized with the sand and waves on either side of the emoticon. Overall, this emoticon is perfect for conveying a carefree and happy-go-lucky mood, ideal for when you want to share your vacation pictures or just spread some good vibes among your friends and family. So, whenever you're feeling like taking a break and hitting the beach, remember to bring along Mr.Ozzy, the vacationing otter!

Design Approach

Here is a translation of the content about my emoticon design process: When starting to design emoticons, I began by researching the most popular symbols and how they were designed to convey messages. I learned about how these symbols could be used to express emotions and feelings, and I chose symbols that I thought would be used the most.

Next, I started drawing the symbols in my own style, using graphic design tools. I used different colors and shapes to create unique and recognizable symbols.

Once I had completed my symbols, I tested them on different platforms to ensure that they were displayed correctly on all devices. I used the symbols in conversations, sent them to friends and colleagues to gather feedback and improve them.

Finally, I created a collection of unique and personal symbols, and I am very pleased with the result. I hope that these symbols will convey my emotions and feelings to users.
